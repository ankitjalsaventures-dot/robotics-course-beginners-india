# Robotics Course for Beginners in India (2026)

Robotics is one of the fastest-growing technologies in the modern world. It combines electronics, programming, and mechanical engineering to build intelligent machines.

This guide helps students understand robotics courses in India, what skills they will learn, and career opportunities in this field.

## What is Robotics?
Robotics is the process of designing, building, and programming robots to perform tasks automatically. It is widely used in industries like manufacturing, automation, healthcare, and artificial intelligence.

## What You Will Learn
- Basics of robotics and automation  
- Sensors and actuators  
- Programming fundamentals  
- Robot design and development  
- Real-world robotics applications

- Beginners interested in technology
